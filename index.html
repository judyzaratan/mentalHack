Welcome to Project Access

Your one stop shop for:
  1. Comprehensive Mental Health Screening
  2. Educational Resources about Mental Illnesses
  3. Treatment Provider Search
  4. Patient Lived Experience Stories
  5. Community Forums

We provide free, confidential, online and comprehensive mental health screening to adults in the San Francisco Bay Area with mental health concerns and help you find treatment providers, learn more about common mental health conditions that may be affecting you, and connect with members of the community who are going through similar experiences.
