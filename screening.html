  2. Generalized Anxiety Disorder

The treatment of Major Depressive Disorder could include certain types of psychotherapy and antidepressant medications. The treatment of Generalized Anxiety Disorder could include certain types of psychotherapy and antidepressant and anti-anxiety medications. Alcohol Use Disorder can be treated with motivational interviewing, Alcoholics Anonymous (a 12-step group), outpatient psychotherapy, and residential rehabilitation programs. Attention-Deficit/Hyperactivity Disorder is usually treated with medication, but cognitive behavioral therapy, coaching and environmental modfication can all be helpful.
